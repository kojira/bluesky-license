🦋 Bluesky License

Turn your Bluesky identity into a driver's-license-style card.

About / notes
  • Enter a Bluesky handle (e.g. user.bsky.social or a custom domain) or a DID, then press Issue.
  • Reads public data from the AT Protocol AppView (public.api.bsky.app): display name, avatar, posts / followers / following counts, account creation date, and recent-post engagement. No login required.
  • HANDLE shows a green ✓ when the account is verified (custom-domain handle, or a Bluesky trusted-verifier / verified status).
  • This is a fan card for fun, not an official ID.
  • If an avatar host doesn't allow CORS, the avatar may fall back to a placeholder in the downloaded image.
