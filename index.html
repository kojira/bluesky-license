🦋 Bluesky License

Turn your Bluesky identity into a driver's-license-style card.

About / notes
  • Enter a Bluesky handle (e.g. user.bsky.social or a custom domain) or a DID, then press Issue.
  • Reads public AT Protocol data: profile, plus your posts / likes / follows (up to the most recent 1000) and the follower graph. No login required.
  • Stats: Web of Trust (mutual follows), Engagement, Generosity (likes given), Velocity, Streak, Veteran — plus Mileage and peak posting hours (UTC).
  • HANDLE shows a green ✓ when the account is verified (custom-domain handle, or Bluesky verified / trusted-verifier status).
  • This is an unofficial fan card for fun — not affiliated with Bluesky, and not an official ID.
